Did you know that many folks who live in senior care facilities have very few visitors? In fact, recent research suggests that approximately 6o percent of residents may have no visitors at all.
Older folks worry about the same things anyone under the age of 65 do, friendships, the stress of day-to-day life. They just might not have anyone to discuss their concerns with.
Kindness is important, but so is fun. Sometimes, we forget that older people like doing fun things just as much as anyone else.
Did you know that animals can help reduce stress? Just stroking the soft fur of a dog, cat or even guinea pig can make people feel a little bit better.
